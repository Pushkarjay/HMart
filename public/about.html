About HMart

HMart is a hyperlocal marketplace built specifically for college students, allowing them to buy and sell products within their hostels and campus environment.

Our Mission

To empower students with a seamless, trusted platform where they can exchange essential items easily, affordably, and safely within their university ecosystem.

How It Works?

  1. Sign Up - Create an account using your college email.
  2. List or Browse Items - Buy & sell anything from books to electronics.
  3. Connect with Peers - Chat securely & finalize deals.
  4. Safe Transactions - A trusted marketplace within your college.

Why Choose HMart?

  • Exclusive to College Students – Verified user base for a safer experience.
  • No Middlemen, No Extra Fees – Direct peer-to-peer transactions.
  • Hyperlocal Convenience – Buy and sell within the campus community.
  • Eco-Friendly & Budget-Friendly – Promotes sustainability by reusing items.
